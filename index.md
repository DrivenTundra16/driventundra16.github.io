## ğŸ”¥ How It Works

Using WhyTwitter is straightforward:

1. Navigate to `twitter.com` or `x.com`, and you will see the extension's popup.
2. Type your reason for visiting Twitter (must be 5 or more characters).
3. Once you type your intention, you gain access for **5 minutes** in **that tab only**.
4. After 5 minutes, the extension will block access again.
5. If you open a new tab, you will need to state a new reason.
